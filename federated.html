01 · Guarantees

What the aggregator cannot learn, by construction.

Secure aggregation

Only the combined result is visible

The central aggregator sees only the sum of all participants' updates. Each individual site's contribution is cryptographically hidden.

Differential privacy

A measurable privacy budget per site

Differential privacy is a mathematical guarantee on how much any single data record can influence the trained model. Each site has a budget (denoted ε); training stops before it's exhausted.

Resilient to bad actors

Robust if up to a third of sites misbehave

Training continues to converge on a good model even if some participants send corrupted or adversarial updates. Several industry-standard robust methods are available.

Offline-tolerant

Sites can drop out or run slow

The aggregator accounts for late or missing updates automatically. No site's outage stalls the whole training.
