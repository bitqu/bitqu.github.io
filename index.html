01 · Products

Three products. One substrate. Deployed together or alone.

Each product works on its own. Together they form a single platform for organizations that run AI across different regions, hardware, and regulatory environments.

Pillar · Agentic simulations

Rehearse futures before they cost you anything.

Run up to a million simulated agents in a virtual world. Replay any run exactly, branch on any decision, and trace every outcome back to its cause.
Product · Federated learning

Deterministic

Replay any run, bit-for-bit

Every run is reproducible: same starting state, same result, down to each agent's decisions. Logs are signed and tamper-evident.

Counterfactual

Branch at any step

Pause a simulation at any point, change a single input, and keep both versions running side by side. Compare them to see exactly what that change caused.

Attributable

Explain every outcome

Break any outcome down into the contributions of individual agents and their interactions. Ship decisions with an audit trail a regulator will accept.
