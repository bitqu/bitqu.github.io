01 · Architecture

Four layers. One contract: your data does not move unless you say so.

Fabric
Control plane
Policy
Define rules about who can use which data for which purpose. Policy language is compatible with Open Policy Agent (OPA).
Attestation
Every worker proves its identity with a hardware signature before running. Supports Intel SGX, AMD SEV-SNP, and AWS Nitro, the industry-standard confidential-computing technologies.
Audit
Every run produces a signed, tamper-evident record that a regulator can verify independently.
Primitives
Intelligence layer
Simulate
Agentic runtime · up to a million agents · exact replay
Learn
Federated trainer · privacy-preserving · tolerant of offline sites
Schedule
Carbon scheduler · reads live grid data · Kubernetes native
Runtime
Execution
Rust core. Python bindings for PyTorch and JAX. Extensible via WebAssembly (WASM) plugins. Fast, encrypted network transport. Artifacts stored by content hash rather than file path.
Substrate
Your compute
AWS · Google Cloud · Azure · Oracle Cloud · your own Kubernetes cluster · supercomputers (HPC) · edge devices · air-gapped networks
02 · What you get

The parts that matter when you're running intelligence as infrastructure.

01
Policy engine
Spell out which data is allowed to leave which site, in a clear policy language. Rules are enforced automatically when workloads run.
02
Unified observability
One dashboard for simulation runs, training rounds, and carbon metrics. Integrates with the OpenTelemetry standard.
03
Cryptographic lineage
Every input, model, and run is fingerprinted and signed so you can always trace what produced what.
04
Fast to production
Most deployments reach their first production workload in under two weeks.
05
Resilient to bad actors
Training stays on track even if up to a third of participating sites are compromised or malfunctioning, with no loss of model quality.
06
Zero-trust networking
Every workload has its own verified identity. No shared passwords or certificates. All connections are mutually authenticated and encrypted.
07
Billing by project
Track spending by research program or team, not by individual machine. Chargeback reports are audit-ready.
08
Disaster-drill mode
Simulate a regional outage in production without affecting real users. Quarterly resilience reports included.
